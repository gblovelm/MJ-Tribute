Michael Jackson

"The King of Pop"

    Here is a timeline of Michael Jackson's life:

  • Aug. 29 1958 - Born at 12:13 AM
  • Aug. 29 1958 - Born at 12:13 AM as Michael Joseph Jackson in Gary, Indiana, to Katherine Esther Scruse and Joseph Walter Jackson.
  • 1962-63 - Michael becomes a member of the Jackson 5 along with his brothers Tito, Jackie, Jermaine, and Marlon. He is lead singer. Their father, Joe, is their manager. Their first paying gig is at a place called Mr. Luck
  • 1968 - The Jackson 5 sign a contract with Motown records.
  • 1969 - The Jackson 5 has their first hit single “Want You Back.” The debut single sells well over two million copies.
  • Dec. 8, 1969 - Jackson 5 release their debut album Diana Ross Presents The Jackson 5.
  • Oct. 7, 1971 - Michael releases his first solo single “Got To Be There.”
  • Feb. 1972 - Michael gets his first U.S. #1 solo hit with “Ben,” a song from the movie Ben, which is the sequel to the 1971 movie Willard. The song spends a week at the top of the U.S. charts and it is the #20 song for 1972.
  • March 27, 1973 - Michael performs “Ben” at the Academy Awards. The song is nominaded for Best Original Song but loses to “The Morning After” from The Poseidon Adventure.
  • 1978 - Has movie debut as a scarecrow in The Wiz, an all-black remake of The Wizard of Oz. It co-stars Diana Ross, Lena Horne, Nipsey Russell and Richard Pryor.
  • Aug. 25, 1979 - His album Off the Wall, produced by Quincy Jones, launches him to superstar standing. It sells over nineteen million copies worldwide.
  • 1980 - Wins a Grammy for Best Vocal Performance with “Don’t Stop ‘Til You Get Enough.”
  • 1981 - He wins two American Music Awards – Favorite Soul/R&B Album and Favorite Soul/R&B Male Artist.
  • March 31, 1981 - He meets Brooke Shields at the Academy Awards and the two begin a relationship.
  • Dec. 1, 1982 - Releases Thriller which sells over 50 million copies worldwide and solidifies him as the King of Pop. It remains today the best selling album of all time.
  • November 1983 - Michael and his brothers sign a $5 million partnership with Pepsi that is more than any other celebrity endorsement deal in history.
  • Nov. 20, 1984 - Gets a star on the Hollywood Walk of Fame.
  • 1985 - He buys all of the rights to The Beatles catalog.
  • Mid 1987 - He gets another nose job and all of a sudden has deep cheekbones and his skin has turned white.
  • Aug. 31, 1987 - He releases the album Bad which sells over eight million copies.
  • May 1988 - Moves to the Neverland Valley Ranch in California’s Santa Ynez Valley.
  • Mid 1996 - While he performs “Earth Song” at the UK Brit awards, and is surrounded by a choir of children, Pulp lead singer Jarvis Cocker storms the stage to protest Jackson portraying Jesus Christ. Jackson is not amused and says he is “sickened, saddened, shocked, upset, cheated and angry.”
  • 2001 - He is inducted into the Rock and Roll Hall of Fame.
  • Nov. 19, 2002 - Dangles his newborn son, Prince Michael II, from a Berlin hotel balcony, four stories high.
  • Nov. 20, 2003 - He is taken into custody by the Santa Barbara County Sherriff’s Department on charges of child molestation. He posts a three million dollar bail.
  • June 13, 2005 - The jury finds Jackson not guilty.
  • June 25, 2009 - He dies of cardiac arrest due to acute propofol and benzodiazepine intoxication. The Los Angeles County Coroner rules his death a homicide. His personal physician Conrad Murray is convicted of involuntary manslaughter.
